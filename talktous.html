Talk to us

Communicate with us and experts to get support whenever you feel down!

What's wrong?

Remain anonymous and talk to us about what's been bothering you. Let us be your safe space. We truly want to help. Your submissions will be taken to our forum, where you will be able to see answers from us and the Mental Space team.

Useful Hotlines

Struggling? Get free, confidential support from a real human over phone, text or webchat. (24H)

  • 211 - If you need assistance locating long-term mental health resources, talking through a problem, or exploring mental health treatment options, call 211 to speak with a live person who can help.

  • 988 - Suicide and Crisis Lifeline - 988's trained counselors will listen, understand how your problems are affecting you, provide support, and connect you to resources if necessary.

  • 1-868-488-7386 - The Trevor Project - If you are thinking about harming yourself — get immediate support. Connect to a crisis counselor, 100% free. (Only in the U.S.)

  • 741741 - Crisis Text Line - This line can help you deal with self-harm. Immediately text a Crisis Counselor when dealing with a self harm crisis.

Take Our Depression Test

Our short depression test's goal is to determine whether you might be experiencing depression. The questions relate to life experiences, common among people who have depression. The test is 100% free for everyone to take. Please do not consider this test as a diagnosis.
